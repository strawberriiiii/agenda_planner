Add a new Day

At first, click on the "Add a new Day" - button on the "Agenda Planner" page. If you want to set the start time of the day, then click on the arrow above or under the first number to change the hour. Then click on the arrow above or under the second number to change the minutes. Finally, click on the "Add a new Day" - button.


Delete a Day

To delete a day, you can click on the "Delete Day" - button of the day, you want to delete, on the "Agenda Planner" page. A small window will open and ask you, if you really want to delete this day. If you confirm, then the day will be deleted.


Add a new Activity

At first, click on the "Add Activity" - button on the "Agenda Planner" page. A small window will open. Then enter the name and the duration time for the activity. Then select the type and add a description for it. Finally, you can save the activity by clicking on the "Submit" - button. To cancel the process, click on the "Cancel" - button.


Edit an Activity

In order to edit an activity, you need to double-click on one of the activities - either in the parked activities in the sidebar, or the activities listed under each day. A window almost identical to the 'add activity' window will appear, with the fields already filled in with the old values. Feel free to change whichever values you like, then just click "Save" and the activity will be updated.


Delete an Activity

To delete an activity, you can click on the dustbin next to the duration time of the activty, which you want to delete. A small window will open and ask you, if you really want to delete this activity. If you confirm, then the activity will be delete.


Add an Activity to a Day

Click the activity, you want to add to the day, and hold it pressed. Then drag it to the day you want to add it to and drop it.


Move an Activity

It is possible to move activites from the sidebar to a day, between days, within a day or from a day to the sidebar backwards. To move an activity, click it and hold it pressed. Then drag it to the dropzone, where you want to move it, and drop it.


Sort Activites in the Sidebar

To sort activities in the sidebar, you can use one of the predefined sorting algorithms. The first button , sorts the activties in alphabetical order. The second button , sorts the activities reverse alphabetical order. The third button , sorts the activities by the type of the activity.
